Welcome to PyLadies Singapore

PyLadies is an international group focused on getting more women into the Python community and programming in general.

This is the official website of PyLadies SG, the Singapore chapter of PyLadies.

Join us on Facebook

If you would like to be notified about upcoming workshops and interesting tech events, just join our Facebook group.

It is an open group and everyone is invited to share links and ask questions about Python, programming and upcoming local events.

Join us on Slack

We also have a growing Slack community. You can join us here and ask us anything if you need help with your Python programming.

Find our recent slides

Have a look at our Resources page to find out more about our past workshops. You will find links to our slides there.

Help us to grow this website

This website is hosted on GitHub. If you find typos or if you have ideas for content that should be featured here, please open an issue or even a pull request on GitHub.
